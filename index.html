Dr. Monika Mittal

Author | Consultant Physician | Mentor

Dr. Monika Mittal (M.B.B.S, M.D. Medicine, EPGM IIM Jammu) is a consultant physician with over 20 years of experience in clinical practice and non-invasive cardiology. She is the author of The Winning Mindset for IIT JEE and the founder of Mentor and Mom, an initiative dedicated to mentoring students and parents for academic and holistic success. As a proud mother of two IITians, she brings a unique blend of professional expertise and personal experience to her mentoring work.

Featured Book
The Winning Mindset for IIT JEE

Co-authored by Dr. Monika Mittal and her two IITian sons, this book goes beyond academics to focus on strategies, mindset, and holistic preparation for IIT JEE. It features 34 actionable tips and 15 inspirational stories from toppers, making it an essential guide for aspirants and parents alike.
